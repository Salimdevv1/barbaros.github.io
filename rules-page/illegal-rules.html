- Kol gang 3andha color el mask mte3ha

- ay player yesta3mel esm wala mask el gang mte3ek 3andek 3lih reason

- ay member bech ya3mel activity illegal lazem ykoun labes el mask officiel

- kif traja3 3la action traja3 mara barka .

- matnajamch tejbed sle7 abyeth 3la chkoun hez 3lik sle7 cartouche ken fi west fight .

- 3andek l7a9 fi max 2 rahina fel robbery

- fel tafaweth m3a l hakem , kol rahina = talab we7ed

- ki yousel l hakem lel robbery 3andek 2 min max bech to5rej tetfaweth m3ah w tnajem totleb menou yzidek wa9t tetfehmou 3lih 3la chakl talab .

- ki tothreb el rahina b sle7 wala to9tlou el hakem ycartech 3lik direct .

- mamnou3 3lik tjib rahina fake .

- ki tabda tetfaweth w to9reb barcha lel police 3andhom l7a9 ychedouk ya3mlou bik echange m3a l rahina w ki y9areb police wahdou zeda tnajem thezou rahina

- fi ay robbery bel rahina lezmek a9al mel police b 2 w ay robbery menghir rahina a9al b 1
