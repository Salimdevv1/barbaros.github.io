Citizen Interaction

- el police lezem dima y5af 3la 7yet l mouwaten w ye7mih 9bal kol chay .
- ay player 3amel mou5alfa teb3a el sye9a t3adilou 5teya 3la blastou .
- ma3andekch l7a9 tetsaref m3a l mouwaten wala tahki m3ah kayenou mojrem ken matabda thebta 3lih el tohma .
- mamnou3 t9alel e7tiram ay mouwaten si nn tetared mel khedma w tousel hata l ban .
- mamnou3 testa3mel el 9owa (7ata bel klem) m3a ay mouwaten si nn tetared mel khedma w tousel hata l ban .
- ay player mehouch recherchee wala ya3mel fi haja illegal wa9tha tetsaref m3ah kima el mouwaten el 3adi hata ken B3 mte3ou mas5a .
- mamnou3 testa3mel ay naw3 mta3 9owa fel scenet akther men 9imet el scene .
- mamnou3 tejbed ay naw3 mta3 sle7 3la mouwaten mouch 3amel chay .
- 3andek l7a9 ta3mel controle papiers m3a ay mouwaten .
- mamnou3 ta3ti ay item/sle7/info/vehicle mta3 police l ay player .
- mamnou3 to5rej 3la charactere l police w inty on duty m3a ay player hata ken sahbek .
- matnajamch thez ay mowaten mehouch majboud wala mochtabah fi 9atheya bessif 3lih lel markez .

Criminal Interaction

- lezem tched el criminal 7ay w matesta3melch l cartouche ken fi 7alet mathel 5tar 3lik wala 3la 7yet el mouwatnin.
- el police mayetfaweth m3a l mjarma ken ki yabda fama rahina
- 3andek l7a9 testa3mel l 9owa (7ata bel klem) m3a l mjarma kahaw w ken f geol w ken inty eli bdit tet7amel mas2oulitek rp (ywali 3andou 3lik reason bech ya5tfek wala yebrakik)
- mamnou3 tathreb wala t3atheb chkoun b 7thour mouwaten 3adi .
- mamnou3 tna7i l ma7jouz l ay 3abd ken f geol .
- mamnou3 to5rej mel markez bel ma7jouz , lezem t7otou fi blastou 9bal .

Police/EMS/Judge interaction

- el radio ken mta3 info w ay klem 5arej l mawthou3 wala tfadlik wala troll tetared 3lih w tousel hata l ban .
- mamnou3 troll w tfadlik on duty sinn tetared 3lih w tetbana .
- lezem 3lik tasma3 ordre ay grade a3la menek mahma kenet ken fi 7alet fama fail fel rules .
- ay 9be7a m3a grade a3la menek 3leha ban direct .
- a3la grade mawjoud fel mdina howa eli ya3ti les ordres (mahma kenet gradou)
- lezem dima fama e7tiram w respect binek w bin ay zamil mahma kenet gradou .
- lezem 3lik dima t3amel l EMS w Judge b e7tiram ki yabdew on duty .
- tnajem mat5afech 3la 7yet zamilek fel Police ki yabda on duty ama dima t5af 3la 7yet el EMS w Judge kima l mouwatnin .
- ay Police/EMS/Judge off duty yet7seb mouwaten 3adi .

Searching Rules

- l police ynajem yfarksek ki tabda fi >danger zone / fel markez / blaset signal robbery/ blaset drogues / lebes mask / 3amel houroub men dawreya / 3awent mojrem b ay tari9a / police dog cham 3lik 7aja illegal / f zone signal hroub mel 7abs / jet 3lik info eli inty fou9ek 7aja illegal / no9tet taftich / player recherche - fi 7alet fama shootout , tnajem ta3mel check status l ay wehed f zone athika w tfarkes ken eli y5arajlek 3andou gunpowder fi 7weyjou .
- ki tfarkes 3abd w tal9a 3andou 7aja illegal tcuffih w thezou tna7ihelou ken fel markez .
- ma3andekech l7a9 tfarkes bel Police Dog menghir reason .
- ma3andekch l7a9 tfarkes ay karhba menghir reason .

Arrest Rules

- ma3andekch l7a9 t5ali mouwaten fel markez akther men 30min (inclus ta79i9) .
- ma3andekch l7a9 t5ali mojrem fel markez akther men 60mn (inclus ta79i9) .
- ma3andekch l7a9 tab3ath ay player 7abs menghir matsawrou w t9ayadlou fel mdt (B3)
- ma3andekch l7a9 tab3ath ay player 7abs ken matna7ilou el ma7jouz .
- ay player 3andou l7a9 yotleb mou7ami fel markez , testana 5min ken majech tkamel el scene
- el mdt matekteb feha ken el 7ajet el s7i7a w mamnou3 tfasa5 menha wala testa3malha l masla7tek cha5seya wala t5arej menha ay info .
- ma3andekch l7a9 tna7i ay item legal (mekla , me , materiels 5edma , etc ..)
- ki tabda mwa9ef player deye5 w bech thezou l sbitar mathezou ken may9olek el EMS tnajem w fi 7alet famech EMS t5alih yarte7 5 minutes .
- fi 7alet de5lek chkoun fel geol w matnajamch thezou lel sbitar tnajem t3ayet l EMS yfay9ou fel markez .
- ma3andekch l7a9 t3adi player mehich thebta 3lih el tohma (tnajem totleb ma7kma)
- ma3andekch l7a9 twa9ef chkoun 3la info jetek ken matthabet menha w tatla3 s7i7a .
- tnajem te7jez vehicle mojrem fi 7alet mou3ayna fel RP .

Pursuit Rules

- mode pursuite s+ testa3mlou ken m3a l kraheb ki tfout 220km .
- mamnou3 testa3mel cartouche fi pursuite ken ki yabda fama 5tar 3la 7yetek wala d5al fi zamil (3la sa9ih wala bel moto).
- l hakem 3andou l 7a9 ya3mel barrage/chouk fi kayes ken ba3d matabda el pursuite w b ordre men a3la grade w lezem yetna7a ba3d el pursuite .
- ba3d 5 minutes mel pursuite , l police 3andou l7a9 yesta3mel EMP gun reload wahda kol 1 minute w el grappler ba3d 10 minutes .
- ma3andekch l7a9 tod5el fi chkoun bel karhba ama tnajem ta3malou block bel vehicles kif ytaya7 vitesse 3al e5er wala karhabtou te9ef wala d5al fi thneya msakra .
- fi 7alet vehicle mta3 l player we9fet w mahbatch menha ki 9otlou tnajem testa3mel teaser wala shotgun mattati .
- ma3andekch l7a9 tsaker thneya 9bal matabda el pursuite .
- fel pursuite l hakem 3andou l7a9 fi 4 vehicles max wra kol vehicle illegal .
- fi ay scene illegal l police 3andou l7a9 y5arej helo wa7da (appart Red alert wala el scene tetaleb akther men helo)
- lezem 3lik t7ot el sirene ki tabda mechi l signal cartouche

Hostage Rules

- fi 7alet fama rahina, 7yetou aham men enek tched el mojrem .
- kol rahina = talab we7ed .
- el police 3andou l7a9 ya3mel fachal moufawthat fi 7alet talab mouch ma39oul wala troll wala rahina met wala tethrab b sle7
- lezem dima fama 1 police minimum yrawa7 bel rahina w ye5ou el evidence .
- fi 7alet tafaweth lezem 3lik t5ali lel mjarma safe passage lel kraheb mte3hom .
- fi 7alet tafaweth a3la grade howa eli yetfaweth wala y3ayen chkoun .
- ki to9reb lel mjarma fel tafaweth men 7a9hom yhezouk rahina w ki yo9reblek el mojrem men 7a9ek thezou bech ta3mel bih echange .
- mamnou3 3lik to9reb lel tafaweth wahdek ken wselt lel robbery menghir da3m .
- ay robbery wala signal menghir tafaweth lezmek temchilou direct menghir matestana l ordre .
- ki yabda fama 5tar 3la 7yetek fi robbery ta3ti info totleb da3m w testana .

Shooting Rules

- ma3andekch l7a9 testa3mel cartouche fi Safe Zone .
- ay player mayhezech ydih 3andek l7a9 ta3melou escort direct w ki yabda 3andou sle7 3andek l7a9 tkartech 3lih
- 3andek l7a9 testa3mel cartouche fi 7alet rit sle7 w mafamech rahina .
- fi fachal moufawthat ma3andekch l7a9 tcartech ken mate5ou ordre a3la grade .
- ay kartouche menghir reason = ban .
- fi 7alet fama fight fel mdina tnajem teda5el direct .
- fi 7alet fama fight f nord lezem testana el fight toufa w ba3d teda5el .
- aandek l7a9 tcartech 3la ay player sera9 vehicle Police .
- aandek l7a9 tcartech 3la ay player hereb mel 7abs.
- tnajem testa3mel cartouche fi pursuite m3a helo wala bateau ba3d 5 minutes
